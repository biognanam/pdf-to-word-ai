# SOP: Research_Section_Management.pdf

## Purpose
Define a repeatable process to convert source PDFs into enterprise-ready workflows.

## Scope
Applies to 2 page(s) and cross-functional operations/documentation teams.

## Procedure
1. **Validate incoming PDF integrity and metadata.** - Validate incoming PDF integrity and metadata.
2. **Extract text, layout, and tabular sections.** - Extract text, layout, and tabular sections.
3. **Generate structured workflow and SOP output.** - Generate structured workflow and SOP output.
4. **Review, approve, and export final deliverables.** - Review, approve, and export final deliverables.

## Quality Controls
- Confirm section-level traceability from source content.
- Validate extracted data with domain reviewer approval.
- Track export artifacts in audit logs.

## Exception Handling
Escalate low-confidence extraction (<85%) to manual QA before release.
