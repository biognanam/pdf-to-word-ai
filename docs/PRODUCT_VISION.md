# Product Vision

## Purpose
Canberbyte DocFlow AI converts unstructured PDF documents into validated, structured, and exportable operational workflows so teams can move faster with less manual documentation effort.

## Target Users
- Enterprise operations teams managing SOP-heavy processes.
- Shared service/documentation teams handling high PDF volume.
- Compliance, quality, and transformation teams requiring traceable document-to-workflow conversion.

## Vision Statement
Build the most reliable and extensible AI-ready platform for transforming PDF knowledge into workflow intelligence that can be reviewed by humans, consumed by systems, and scaled across organizations.
